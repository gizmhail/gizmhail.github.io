Primezone Cup 2013 : ManetteTV (TVCommande d’Orange)

Context: Orange (2013)

Primezone Cup is an internal contest, where developer can create and publish their mobile application ideas.

ManetteTV (later called TV Commande d'Orange) was an application to control remotly Orange TV STBs, which also displayed live programs to act both as a classical and as a more live remote.
