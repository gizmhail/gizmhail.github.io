Orange Innovation Cup 2022 : Friends Moments

Context: Orange (2021)

Orange Innovation Cup is an internal contest, where developer can create and publish their application ideas. The 2022 session started a new category, for XR apps, which we won with Friends Moments.

Friends Moments is an Oculus Quest VR application to play with friends and spend good times together.
