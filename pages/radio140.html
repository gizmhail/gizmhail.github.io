Radio 140

Skills: iOS, Twitter, T2S
Context: Orange iOS developer (2013 - 2015)
Link: Article on the app

Radio 140 was allowing to listen to your Twitter feed "eye-free", played as a radio. It was then possible to safely keep up to date with twitter while walking, cooking, ...

The app was vocalising the user twitter timeline, using the iOS integrated speech to text.
Besides, the application was reading a preview of the website whose links were found inside the tweets.
If desired, you can then either add the website to your safari read later list, or vocalise the full webpage content directly, with limited interactions to keep the users safe.

Other than the timeline, it was possible to read predetermined contents, twitter lists, or a specific search result.
Role: Base idea, Main iOS developper, Conception
