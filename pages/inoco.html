iNoco

Skills: iOS
Context: Personal product (2014 - April 8th 2018) Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3hxEaEjaYcM

iOS application to access of the french TV channel Nolife's VOD catalog, noco.tv (now closed). After authorisation of the TV channel, I was allowed to publish this app and handle its support on my behalf.

I shared the core iOS library to manipulate the noco.tv API, so that anyone could build another client (https://github.com/gizmhail/NoLibTV)
