TVCommande d'Orange (previously ManetteTV)

Skills: iOS, STB, support, communication
Context: Orange iOS developer (2013 - 2015)
Link: Demonstration video, Article about this product

TVCommande d'Orange was a remote control iOS application to control Orange TV set-top-boxes. Aside from the basic TV remote commands, it offered a live electronic program guide, with the ability to see, search, select, filter and push a program to the TV, based on its title, channel or themes.
It also offered an Apple Watch version to control the TV directly from the user's watch, or a virtual gamepad in the iPhone and iPad version to control Orange's game streaming service.

Created during an innovation contest, it attracted more than a million users, and was then merged in the official Orange mobile TV application.

Role : Main iOS developer, Conception, Communication, Support (more than 8k mails with the users when running the support alone)
